SoCIT Fest

  • by:Alliah Bautista
  • date:June 2019
  • 1 Comment

SoCIT Fest started August 14 and ended August 16. I am one of the organizers of it, and together with my fellow SoCIT organizations, I can say we had a blast SoCIT Fest. I am from Junior Philippine Computer Society – APC (JPCS-APC), and our events are the Agile Seminar, UI Design Competition, Fame or Shame, and SoCIT Night in collaboration with JISSA, MSC, and Gaming Genesis.

Agile Seminar’s purpose is to help to prepare the students to deliver value faster with greater quality and predictability and greater aptitude to respond to change.  The GDAP event that was held last August 15 at MpH1 was hosted by Gaming Genesis, but other SoCIT Orgs helped in preparing and facilitating.

As part of the organizer of the SoCIT Fest, it was overwhelming when I saw the audience or participants where enjoying the event. We’ve also received compliments from the directors about the collaboration of the other SoCIT organization. All those hard work and stressful days to prepare this big event, it was all worth it and paid off. I have to go through the rough path in order to commit success. But I believe there are still a lot of things to improve my skills and our event. This is just the start.

For the next school year’s SoCIT Fest, I expect that the collaboration and camaraderie of the SoCIT Organization are still bonded. Also, for the future events, more interactive and lively that would cater all or most of the SoCIT Students.
